Introduction

At THE ATELIER, we believe that luxury is not just about the quality of our garments, but the integrity of our relationship with you. This Privacy Policy outlines how we treat your personal information with the same level of care and bespoke attention we apply to our collections.

By engaging with our digital platform, you entrust us with your data. This document serves as our commitment to transparency, detailing what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your digital footprint.

Information Collection

A. Identity & Contact

We collect essential identification data such as your full name, billing and shipping addresses, email, and telephone number when you create an account or finalize a commission.

B. Payment & Transactional

To facilitate secure acquisitions, we process payment details through encrypted gateways. This includes transaction history and purchase preferences to better tailor our future offerings.

C. Technical & Browsing

We automatically collect data regarding your interaction with our digital atelier—IP addresses, device metadata, and navigation patterns—to refine the user experience.

Utilization of Data

We process your personal information under the following legal bases:

  • check_circle Operational Fulfillment: To process orders, deliver garments, and manage your Atelier account.
  • check_circle Curation & Personalization: To suggest pieces from our collections that align with your aesthetic history.
  • check_circle Service Evolution: To analyze store performance and improve the fluidity of our digital interface.

Third-Party Engagement

THE ATELIER does not sell, lease, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes. We share data only with trusted partners who assist in our operations:
